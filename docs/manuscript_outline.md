## Abstract (~250 words)

**Background:** [1-2 sentences on the rise of short-form video content and concerns about explicit content exposure]

**Objective:** This study examines user perceptions and concerns regarding explicit content (nudity, vulgarity) in short-form video platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels) and their reported impacts on mental health across different age groups in Nepal—a developing digital economy.

**Methods:** We analyzed [N] publicly available user reviews from Google Play Store and Apple App Store using sentiment analysis (VADER), topic modeling (LDA), and thematic content analysis. Reviews were coded for explicit content mentions, age-related concerns, and mental health impacts.
